FaceRecognitionMode

Recognition mode to configure FaceAnalyzer to.

  • disabled

    Configures FaceAnalyzer to disable recognition.

  • verifyMatchToFaceIn(singleFaceImage:)

    Configures FaceAnalyzer to verify against the largest face seen in given VisionSource.

  • verifyMatchToFaceInSessionConfiguration

    Configures FaceAnalyzer to verify against a preset face within session configuration.
